Grand Sunday Buffet

Every Sunday, Guarda Golf Hotel & Residences hosts the unmissable gourmet event of the Crans-Montana, the Grand Sunday Buffet. You will be amazed by the number of mouthwatering dishes served in beautiful glassware, the choice of succulent meats and fishes and the assortment of desserts. The difficulty is simple: where to begin?

The Sunday Buffet is available from 12:30 to 2:30pm every Sunday while the hotel is open, for CHF 85.- per adult and CHF 45.- per child.
